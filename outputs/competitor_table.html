Competitor Comparison Table

Company Positioning Risk
Luckin Coffee Luckin dominates the value-oriented mass coffee market and relies on scale, digital operations, and aggressive pricing. Strong price competition and high customer acquisition costs in top-tier cities.
Starbucks China Starbucks China focuses on premium positioning and experience-led consumption in major metropolitan markets. Premium-only expansion is difficult because major metropolitan areas are already saturated.
Cotti Coffee Cotti Coffee uses an asset-light franchise model and rapid expansion strategy, especially suitable for lower-tier city penetration. Franchise expansion without strong local execution and compliance control can create operational instability.
